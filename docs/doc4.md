# Data Processing Consent Requirements

## Consent Definition
Consent under Decree 13/2023/ND-CP must be:
- Freely given
- Specific and informed
- Unambiguous
- Documented
- Revocable at any time

## When Consent is Required
Explicit consent is required for processing:
- Sensitive personal data
- Cross-border data transfers to non-adequate countries
- Automated decision-making
- Direct marketing
- Any processing not covered by other legal bases

## Consent Form Requirements
Consent forms must include:
- Clear description of data processing purposes
- Categories of personal data to be processed
- Identity of the data controller
- Duration of consent
- Rights of the data subject
- Consequences of refusing consent
- Contact information for inquiries

## Special Cases
- **Children's Data**: Consent from legal guardians required for children under 16
- **Employee Data**: Can use employment contract as legal basis instead of consent
- **Public Interest**: Government agencies can process without consent for public purposes
- **Vital Interests**: Processing without consent allowed to protect life

## Consent Management
Data controllers must:
- Implement consent management systems
- Allow easy withdrawal of consent
- Keep records of consent history
- Verify consent authenticity
- Update consent when processing purposes change
